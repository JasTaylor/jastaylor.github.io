About... Me!

Hi there! My name is Jasmine Taylor and thanks for visiting my portfolio. I moved to Boston about 2.5 years ago from Virginia after traveling solo around Europe for 4 months. After 7 years in the healthcare industry, I became a Software Engineer so that I could pursue something that I absolutely loved doing. I believe that software engineering will be a career that allows me to positively impact the community around me while allowing me to problem solve creatively.

When I am not practicing my skills or working on a new project, I love taking photos on my digital camera, finding new ramen places around Boston, and spending quality time with my dog Charlie!
