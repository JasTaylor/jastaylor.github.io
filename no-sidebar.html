About... Me!

Hi there! My name is Jasmine Taylor and thanks for visiting my portfolio. A southerner at heart, I moved to Boston from Richmond, Va in 2017 and haven't looked back since. I have a B.S. in Healthcare and Business Adminstration from James Madison University and have previsouly worked in the healthcare industry for 7 years before switching into software development in 2019.

When cultivated with passion and hardwork, software engineering is an art that can create access to services and products that help make the world a better place. While I love software development because of the challenge and its constantly changing landscape, I have found that it is most rewarding when building a product that directly helps others.

When I am not practicing my skills or working on a new project, I love taking photos on my Nikon, finding new ramen places around Boston, painting, traveling, cycling, and spending quality time with my bichon Charlie!
